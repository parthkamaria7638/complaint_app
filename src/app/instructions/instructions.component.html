Welcome to Complaints App for citizens!

This page includes instructions for registering your complaints through various social media platforms and this website. You can also find instructions on how to track your complaints once accepted. You can also find information on how to contact the personnel assigned to your complaint thereby increasing reachability and transparency

Formatting your complaints:

  1. For Reddit and Facebook

    Location: Your Complaint Location
    Body: You Complaint Body

  2. For Twitter

    Location: Your Complaint Location
    Type: Complaint Type. Must be one of "Water", "Road", "Sanitation", "RTO"
    Body: You Complaint Body

For registering complaints on this website:

  1. Go to the register complain page here
  2. Fill out all the relevant fields of Email, Name, Location, Type, Body and click Post Complaint
  3. After your complaint has been approved by the complaints manager, you will receive and Email with the tracking link

For registering complaints on Reddit:

  1. Go to the complaints app subreddit
  2. Create a post with a relevant title and put your complaint in the body with the format described above
  3. If the format was correct, you will get a confirmation comment on your post, otherwise, your post will be removed by the moderator
  4. When the complaints manager approves your complaint, you will get another comment with the tracking link for your complaint

For registering complaints on Facebook:

  1. Go to the complaints receiver Facebook Page Page
  2. Click on send message. This will open a popup where you can talk to the complaint receiver
  3. Write your complaint in the appropriate format and send it as a message here
  4. If your complaint was formatted properly, you will receive a confirmation message. If not, you will receive a message asking you to resend your message in the proer format
  5. When the complaints manager approves your complaint, you will get another message with the tracking link for your complaint

For registering complaints on Twitter:

  1. For registering a complaint on Twitter, you can send a message to @ComplaintRecei1. Alternatively, you can tag the complaints receiver in your tweets to register your complaints
  2. If your complaint was formatted properly, you will receive a confirmation message/reply. If not, you will receive a message asking you to resend your message in the proer format
  3. When the complaints manager approves your complaint, you will get another message/reply with the tracking link for your complaint
